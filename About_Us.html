HEADSPACE

Mission Statement

“To empower young people to have the confidence and skills to gain meaningful employment.”


Staff

We have 4 full time staff but we do have a large number of associates specialising in a range of employability skills who work for us on a part time, sessional or casual basis.

Our full time staff:

Fiona Hackworth - Manager

Fiona is responsible for the day to day running of our centre as well as the overall strategic direction and future planning. She organises all of our events, recruit’s associates and ensures that everything runs smoothly.

Dan Waterhouse – Outreach manager

Dan works closely with schools, colleges and other youth organisations providing workshops, one to one guidance and general training to staff and young people.

Juanita Marquez – Marketing and promotions

Juanita is responsible for ensuring that we can reach a wider audiences and therefore help more of our young people. She is our event’s organiser and PR officer. She is also responsible for the new website.

Eliza Shaftoe – Counsellor and personal advisor

Eliza is responsible for all of our one to one support and guidance work. Eliza is able to provide very specific and individualised guidance. She also carries out one to one assessment in order to identify strengths and weaknesses and make recommendations on employability skills to be developed.


Aims and Objectives

Our Aims and objectives are to provide and guide young people to gain a meaningful employment.

What makes us unique

We don’t consider ourselves to be in competition with any other services but do believe we are able to provide a more individualised approach to assisting young people into employment than job centres and have more resources to draw upon than traditional schools career advisory teams.
